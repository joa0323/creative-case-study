# Creative Case Study – From Idea to Final Project

## About this project

This started as a simple idea:
"I want to make a pineapple pillow."

While working on it, the idea naturally grew into something much bigger.

Instead of creating one object, I ended up building a full themed scene with characters, decorations and small details.

---

## How I worked

* I started with one simple idea

* Then I broke it down into smaller parts:

  * characters
  * clothing
  * decorations
  * overall scene

* While working, I kept thinking:
  "what else can I add?"
  "how can I improve it?"

* The project developed step by step into a complete composition

---

## What this shows about me

Even though this is a creative project, it reflects how I think:

* I naturally break things into parts
* I expand ideas into structured solutions
* I pay attention to details
* I like improving things step by step

---

## Why this matters for QA

This is similar to how I approach testing:

* breaking systems into smaller parts
* thinking about different scenarios
* improving step by step
* paying attention to small details
