# Creative Case Study – From Idea to Final Project

## 🧠 About this project

This started as a simple idea:
👉 "I want to make a pineapple pillow."

But as I was working on it, the idea started to grow and evolve into something much bigger.

Instead of one object, I created a full themed scene with characters, decorations and details.

---

## 🔍 How I worked

* I started with one simple idea

* Then I broke it down into smaller elements:

  * characters
  * clothing
  * decorations
  * overall scene

* While working, I kept asking:
  👉 "what else can I add?"
  👉 "how can I make it more complete?"

* The project evolved step by step into a full composition

---

## 💡 What this shows about me

Even though this is a creative project, it reflects how I think:

* I naturally break things into parts
* I expand ideas into structured solutions
* I pay attention to details
* I enjoy improving things step by step

---

## 🔄 Why this matters for QA

This is similar to how I approach testing:

* breaking systems into parts
* thinking about different scenarios
* improving step by step
* paying attention to small details
